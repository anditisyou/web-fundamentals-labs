How to Register for Shine Hospital OPD Booking @ors.gov.in?

In this section, we will share the step-by-step procedure to Register for OPD Booking at the ORS Patient Portal. Follow the below process:-

  • Go to the Official Portal of the Online Registration System (ORS), i.e. https://ors.gov.in/
  • Click on the Register/Login tab under the menu bar on the web homepage.
  • You will be redirected to the Registration page. Here you have two options;
  • Using Mobile Number
  • Using ABHA (Health ID)
  • In the first one, you have to enter the Mobile Number and Captcha Code.
  • Then click on the ‘Get OTP’. An OTP will be sent to your mobile number.
  • Enter the OTP and you will be logged in. Now, you have to register the family members.
  • Keep in mind that, the user can only register 6 family members using one number.
  • In the second one, you have to first create the ABHA (Health ID) by clicking on the Create It link.
  • After that, you can log in using ABHA Health ID. Please enter the details correctly.
